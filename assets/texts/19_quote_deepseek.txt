Despite Italy's rich history in technology and gaming, there has been a lack of recognition for key Italian innovations. From Olivetti's pioneering work in computing to the iconic "Programma 101," Italy's contributions have significantly influenced global tech, yet they remain underappreciated. This episode delves into why these contributions are overlooked and the implications of this neglect.

Preserving the history of technology is a critical task. With obsolete technologies becoming increasingly rare, there is an urgent need to safeguard Italy's contributions before it's too late. Olivetti and the "Programma 101" are emblematic of Italy's technological ingenuity. These innovations not only advanced computing but also laid the groundwork for future developments. Understanding their impact provides valuable insights into the evolution of technology.
